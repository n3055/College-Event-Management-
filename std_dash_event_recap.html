Event Recap: Annual Cultural Fest 2025

Event Highlights

The Annual Cultural Fest 2025 was a grand celebration of creativity, talent, and unity. With many clubs participating, the campus buzzed with energy through dance performances, musical nights, art exhibits, and theatrical acts.

Key Moments

  • Dance event by Nrityatrix
  • Open Mic by Saahitya and Toastmasters
  • Battle of Bands hosted by Dhwani
  • Live painting event by Aakriti
  • Award ceremony for top performances

We thank all the volunteers and participants who made this fest unforgettable. Let’s carry this spirit forward to the next year!
